She can survive in deep space for long periods of time without a [[spacesuit]] or viable [[atmosphere]], by generating a self-sustaining environmental forcefield. In deep space, she can travel at faster-than-light speeds. In an Earth-normal atmosphere or in a sizable gravity, her speed is limited by friction & drag.\n\n==Equipment==\nAs a member of the [[Legion of Super-Heroes]], she is provided a Legion Flight Ring.
