Phantazia was able to sense, manipulate, influence, and disrupt various fields along with various forms of energies and energy wavelengths to varying degrees. Though the upper limits of her powers were never clearly defined or fully explored, it is inferred that Phantazia could use her powers and abilities to create various electromagnetic phenomenon.
