He has used this skill to render himself immortal. He also has the powers of mesmerism, thought-casting, illusion-casting, and astral projection.\n\nAccording to the back story of the character, Kaluu is possibly the most powerful living human black magician. He does not age, but he can be killed by external means.\n\nKaluu is a master of various Asian martial arts, but prefers not to engage in physical combat.
