It has limited artificial intelligence and no capacity for self-motivated activity, and is totally dependent on its programming or the programmer\'s spoken commands, and usually deactivates itself when not active.\n\nThe Android has inhuman physical attributes and can mimic an ability (one at a time) after touching an opponent, such as the [[Thing (comics)|Thing]]\'s rock-like epidermis or [[Iceman (comics)|Iceman]]\'s frost coating. It can also emit close-range blasts of [[gale|gale-force wind]] from its mouth.
