However, a confrontation with [[Hawkeye (Clint Barton)|Hawkeye]] (disguised as the [[Golden Archer]]) forces Rogers to realize that he cannot abandon a life of heroism, and he subsequently takes on the name "Nomad" (as it means "man without a country") adopting a new dark blue and yellow uniform with no patriotic markings on it at all.\n\nThis identity is short-lived, with Rogers maintaining it for a mere four issues of the comic to varying degrees of success; he even trips over his own cape at one point.
