Quartermain later became part of the [[U.S. military]]\'s "Hulkbusters" operation, which attempted to capture and contain the [[Hulk]].\n\nHe became a fugitive and traveled with [[Rick Jones (comics)|Rick Jones]] and the Hulk. He met his brother, a farmer. He testified in Congress about a mass murder committed by the [[supervillain]] known as the [[Leader (comics)|Leader]] who had detonated a gamma bomb in a town to obliterate.<ref>\'\'The Incredible Hulk\'\' (vol. 2) #337-343 (November 1987-April 1988)</ref>\n\nOnce more at S.H.I.E.L.D., Quartermain, along with many other agents, was seemingly killed by a self-aware, renegade "Deltan" variety of the agency\'s [[Android (robot)|artificial-human]] "[[Life Model Decoy]]s", and replaced by one such LMD.<ref>\'\'Nick Fury vs. S.H.I.E.L.D.\'\' #2 (July 1988)</ref> The most advanced of the Deltite LMDs, it rebelled upon learning it was not human, and after several confrontations, perished.<ref>\'\'Nick Fury vs. S.H.I.E.L.D.\'\' #6 (November 1988)</ref>\n\n[[Retconned]] as a former romantic interest of [[Jessica Jones]], Quartermain later became leader of [[Nick Fury\'s Howling Commandos|S.H.I.E.L.D.\'s Paranormal Containment Unit]].
