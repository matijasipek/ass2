As with Spider-Man, he is a formidable hand-to-hand combatant though he has had no formal training. He uses a unique type of freestyle fighting that allows him to make full use of his powers. He does not seem to have any web-generating devices like Spider-Man\'s web-shooters, and is never seen to produce any webbing naturally. In his conversations with Peter Parker, the subject never comes up.
