She can also perform psychic surgery and appear to others in spirit (astral) form.  She has a gifted intellect. When dying, she displayed the ability to transfer her mutation to another individual, Julia Carpenter, as well as her blindness.{{issue|date=December 2014}} Madame Web was a victim of \'\'[[myasthenia gravis]]\'\', a disorder of neuromuscular junction transmission.  As a result, she was an invalid, entirely dependent on external, life support for survival. This is no longer the case as she was cured of the condition some time ago. She is also blind.
