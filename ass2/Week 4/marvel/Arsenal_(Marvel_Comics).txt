In "Thanos Rising", Arsenal serves as Iron Man\'s friend and protector while helping the latter and [[Thor (Marvel Comics)|Thor]] fight the [[Grim Reaper (comics)|Grim Reaper]]. When the [[Avengers (comics)|Avengers]] fight [[Thanos]], Arsenal sacrifices itself by self-destructing to send Thanos away from Earth. After recovering its head, the Avengers gradually rebuild and modify Arsenal over the course of "Ghosts of the Past" and "Back to the Learning Hall". In "Thanos Triumphant", Arsenal uses its new form to absorb the [[Infinity Gems|Infinity Stones]]\' energies and help the Avengers defeat Thanos once more.
