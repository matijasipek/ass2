Ka-Zar and his wife [[Shanna the She-Devil]] rescue the baby and discover that Parnival employed Gregor, so Ka-Zar travels to New York City to find out why his brother wanted him killed. Shanna, who had followed Ka-Zar to New York, is then attacked by the Plunderer\'s men.
