Although they failed, Bug stayed with the Guardians as they dealt with the war\'s aftermath in the [[Realm of Kings]] storyline through the series\' end with issue #25 in 2010. Bug appeared as a member of the Guardians in \'\'[[Avengers Assemble (comics)|Avengers Assemble]]\'\' issues #4-8 in 2012.
