With the diode implants, Parks is capable of energy projection, [[light refraction]] for [[invisibility]] and [[illusion]] generation.\n\nParks\'s material body is eventually replaced by photons due to an overloading of the diode implants. By increasing the [[density]] of the photons comprising his form, Parks can achieve "solidity", project photons as energy beams, and create [[three-dimensional]] [[holographic]] images. The character also possesses limited telepathic abilities and can [[Speed of light|travel at light speed]].\n\n==Other versions==\n===What If?===\nThe [[Parallel universe (fiction)|alternate universe]] title \'\'[[What If (comics)|What If]]\'\' features a story which the character reforms and works at Stark Industries, with three different outcomes being presented.<ref>\'\'What If?\'\' vol. 2 #63 (July 1994).
