Tyler, now working with Zero, used his mutant powers to discover the truth about the exact relationship between Cable and Stryfe. He revealed this knowledge to Cable, who was possessed by Stryfe\'s spirit. The truth that Stryfe was a [[Clone (genetics)|clone]] of Cable, was too much for Stryfe to bear and Cable was released, while Stryfe\'s spirit died.
