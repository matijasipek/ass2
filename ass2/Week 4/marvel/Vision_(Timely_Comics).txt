Leaving the prisoner on the planet Jupiter, Aarkus accepts Erickson\'s invitation to fight crime on Earth.<ref>\'\'[[Marvel Mystery Comics]]\'\' #13 (Nov. 1940)</ref> Aarkus does so for several years, although during World War II he is briefly manipulated by the [[Axis powers of World War II|Axis]] powers into fighting the [[Allies of World War II|Allies]]. During the story, he falls into a coma and is confined to the medical wing of the [[Jean Grey School for Higher Learning]].\n\nAfter awaking from his coma, Aarkus is approached by the [[Bucky Barnes|Winter Soldier]] for help in getting the surviving members of the Invaders to the [[Kree]] home world to rescue [[Namor]].
