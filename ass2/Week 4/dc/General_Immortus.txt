This syndicate is militaristic, hence Immortus\' title of "[[general (military)|general]]", and not unlike the [[Thule Society]] in its philosophy and dabbling in the occult. From time to time thereafter, Immortus sparred with the Doom Patrol, usually in an effort to obtain the results of the Chief\'s life-extension research, or the Doom Patrol intercepted Immortus as he attempted to steal ancient mystical artifacts.\n\nImmortus was a one-time member of the [[Brotherhood of Evil]], a group formed largely to combat the Doom Patrol.\n\nA second Doom Patrol was initiated by an [[India]]n woman named Arani, claiming to be Mrs. Niles Caulder and calling herself [[Celsius (comics)|Celsius]].
