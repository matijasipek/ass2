Ferro comes close to heroically sacrificing his life in an effort to stop the Sun-Eater. At the last instant, he is saved by [[Hal Jordan]], who was maintaining the power and identity of [[Parallax (comics)|Parallax]]. Jordan seemingly sacrifices his own life and the sun is restored. Unlike Stone Boy, Ferro Lad had full mobility and could think and speak normally. His iron-like form could resist considerable damage, including direct hits by laser blasts, and he was able to survive unprotected in the vacuum of space. He could use his ability offensively by ramming his iron-hard body into an opponent or an obstacle. His physical strength also increased when in his iron state, allowing him to bend metal bars or reshape scrap metal like clay.\n\n==Equipment==\nAs a member of the [[Legion of Super-Heroes]] he was provided a Legion Flight Ring. It allowed him to fly and protects him from the vacuum of space and other dangerous environments.
