Chemo destroys the alternate universe version of [[New York City]] and kills [[Aquagirl (Tula)|Aquagirl]] by releasing large quantities of toxic chemicals into the ocean. The entity is dispersed and neutralized when the heroine [[Valentina Vostok|Negative Woman]] shatters Chemo\'s plastic shell.<ref>\'\'Crisis on Infinite Earth\'\' #9-10 (December 1985-January 1986)</ref>\n\nDuring the \'\'[[Infinite Crisis]]\'\' storyline, [[Alexander Luthor Jr.]]\'s incarnation of the [[Secret Society of Super Villains]] employs the [[Brotherhood of Evil]] to use Chemo as a living weapon by dropping the entity onto the city of [[Blüdhaven]]. At the same time in the "[[Batman: Under the Hood]]" storyline, [[Batman]] sees Chemo hit Blüdhaven from a rooftop in [[Gotham City]].<ref>\'\'Batman\'\' #649 (January 2006),</ref> After Chemo covers the city with toxic waste, Chemo kills hundreds of thousands of people, but is stopped when Superman throws the entity into deep space.<ref>\'\'Infinite Crisis\'\' #1-7 (December 2005-June 2006)</ref>\n\nDuring the \'\'[[Salvation Run]]\'\' storyline, Chemo is retrieved and used as a weapon by fellow villains the [[Joker (character)|Joker]] and [[Gorilla Grodd]] whilst on a prison planet.<ref>\'\'Salvation Run\'\' #1-7 (November 2007June 2008)</ref> Rogue [[New Gods]] visiting the city of [[Metropolis (comics)|Metropolis]] capture a trio of multicolored miniature versions of Chemo.
