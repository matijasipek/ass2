Once the scheme had run its course, Nocturna called it quits. During this time, she became an intimate friend of both Bruce Wayne and the Batman, something that made both lives more complicated, especially after they had slept together after Bruce lost custody of Jason. She also became a [[surrogate mother]] to [[Jason Todd]], the second [[Robin (character)|Robin]], and even adopted him. Not wanting Bruce out of Jason\'s life she proposed that there was only one sensible solution: she and Bruce must get married at once. Although this seemed to be for his wealth, it is later revealed to be because she is in love with him.\n\nAnton Knight finally escaped and, after being rejected by his beloved Nocturna, changed his style and became the murderous Night-Slayer. Before he could do so, Night-Slayer was captured by [[Catwoman]]. The Spoiler later track Natalia to her apartment and helps her save her scientific research papers when it is discovered that her apartment is on fire.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://batmanytb.com/comics/titles/robin/101_200/robin103.php|title=Robin (vol. 2) #103|publisher=Batman: Yesterday, Today, & Beyond|access-date=2011-01-12}}</ref>\n\nWhile Natalia stays at the Spoiler\'s house, Robin and the Spoiler learn that Natalia secretes a pheromone that causes those around her to become emotional and lose their inhibitions.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://batmanytb.com/comics/titles/robin/101_200/robin104.php|title=Robin (vol. 2) #104|publisher=Batman: Yesterday, Today, & Beyond|access-date=2011-01-12}}</ref> They deduce that the pheromones caused her attacker, a former schizophrenic patient named Dylan Arthur Prescott, to relapse and attack her. When confronted with this information, Natalia denies any knowledge of her powers. She had been an aspiring scientist that eventually quit the scientific field once she contracted her powers, which caused her to lose her inhibitions and pursue other interests (including playing the piano, acquiring a medieval sense of fashion, and other unconventional choices). Robin and the Spoiler convince her to visit [[S.T.A.R. Labs]] to have her powers examined.\n\n[[File:Nocturna-Spoiler.jpg|thumb|right|175px|Nocturna battles her former friend the Spoiler in \'\'Gotham Gazette: Batman Alive?\'\' #1 (July 2009), art by Guy Major.]]\nThe Spoiler and Natalia developed a friendship and the Spoiler shared a great amount of personal information with Natalia while her nose was not plugged. Robin and the Spoiler eventually found her as she was being confronted by Prescott.
